Description of the Course

This is an advanced level course on classical mechanics. The course opens with Newton's laws of motion and systematically develops the dynamics of classical particles,
with chapters on basic principles, rotating coordinate systems, lagrangian formalism, small oscillations, dynamics of rigid bodies, and hamiltonian formalism, including a
brief discussion of the transition to quantum mechanics. Parts of the following subjects may be further covered (if there is enough time and interest): Special Relativity.
Canonical transformations. Hamilton-Jacobi theory. Action- angle variables. Poisson brackets.
